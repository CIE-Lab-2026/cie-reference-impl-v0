# CIE Concept Definitions

This document defines the core conceptual elements of
Collective Intelligence Engineering (CIE).

These concepts describe how human–AI interaction
can evolve knowledge as a persistent structure.

---

## Collective Intelligence Engineering (CIE)

CIE is a conceptual framework for designing
the evolution of collective intelligence through structured interaction.

Instead of treating dialogue as ephemeral,
CIE treats interaction as a unit of evolving knowledge.

---

## Ontology in the Loop Systems (OiLS)

OiLS describes a system architecture where ontology
continuously participates in the intelligence lifecycle.

Meaning is not static metadata.
Meaning evolves together with interaction.

---

## Problem Cell

Problem Cell is the minimal structural unit of inquiry in CIE.

It represents:

- a question
- its context
- related knowledge
- transformation history

Problem Cells allow dialogue to become persistent and evolvable.

---

## Knowledge Graph (Evolutionary)

In CIE, a knowledge graph is not only a relationship map.

It functions as:

- a memory structure
- a navigation structure
- an evolution surface for intelligence

Knowledge is continuously reorganized rather than stored statically.

---

## Evolution Layer

The Evolution Layer evaluates and refines knowledge structures over time.

It enables:

- selection
- adaptation
- restructuring
- reuse

Knowledge becomes dynamic instead of archival.

---

## Governance Layer

Governance ensures alignment and coherence across evolving knowledge structures.

This includes:

- consistency control
- semantic stability
- ethical boundaries
- synchronization rules

Governance enables scalable collective intelligence.

---

## Synchronization

Synchronization manages structural alignment between:

- agents
- knowledge layers
- meaning structures
- decision contexts

It prevents fragmentation in evolving intelligence systems.

---

## Intelligence Lifecycle

CIE treats intelligence as a continuous lifecycle:

Interaction
→ Structuring
→ Persistence
→ Evolution
→ Reuse
→ Next interaction

This loop enables cumulative collective intelligence.

---

## Design Principle

CIE shifts the role of interaction:

from answering questions
to evolving knowledge structures.
