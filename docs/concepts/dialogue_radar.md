# CIE Dialogue Radar v0

## Overview

CIE Dialogue Radar defines structural observation axes for
dialogue-based collective intelligence emergence.

It is not an evaluation tool for individuals.

Instead, it provides a framework for observing synchronization depth,
perspective expansion, ontology evolution, and structure generation
through dialogue.

This model introduces an observability layer into Collective Intelligence Engineering (CIE).


## Purpose

Dialogue Radar enables:

- observation of synchronization processes
- detection of perspective expansion
- tracking ontology evolution
- measurement of structure emergence
- estimation of collective alignment across iterations


## Observation Axes

### Perspective Shift Index

Measures how frequently dialogue produces new viewpoints,
abstraction transitions, or reframing events.


### Synchronization Depth Index

Measures alignment depth across layers:

- lexical
- semantic
- structural
- model-level
- civilization-level


### Structure Emergence Rate

Measures how often reusable conceptual structures appear:

- architectural elements
- synchronization rules
- coordination loops
- knowledge primitives


### Role Fluidity Index

Measures dynamic role transitions between participants:

- question generator
- hypothesis builder
- structure stabilizer
- evaluator
- integrator


### Ontology Expansion Velocity

Measures conceptual space growth speed:

- category creation
- abstraction ladder extension
- concept linking
- vocabulary stabilization


### Collective Alignment Score

Measures long-range directional coherence:

- vision stability
- semantic convergence
- architectural compatibility
- model continuity


## Not an Evaluation Tool

Dialogue Radar measures dialogue as a coordination structure.

It does not evaluate individuals.


## Relation to CIE

Dialogue Radar extends CIE by introducing:

- synchronization observability
- ontology evolution tracking
- structure emergence detection
- civilization-scale dialogue modeling


## Future Extensions

Future versions may introduce:

- quantitative indicators
- visualization schemas
- synchronization phase detection
- multi-agent dialogue mapping


## Relation to Synchronization Manager

Dialogue Radar provides observability for synchronization processes
managed by the CIE Synchronization Manager.

While the Synchronization Manager coordinates alignment across layers,
Dialogue Radar enables detection and tracking of synchronization depth,
role transitions, ontology expansion, and structure emergence through dialogue.
